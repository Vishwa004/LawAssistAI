Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate legal professionals and AI experts, Law Assist AI was born out of the need to democratize legal information. Witnessing how complex and expensive the legal system can be, our founders set out to create an AI-driven platform that simplifies the process and reduces costs for everyone.

Key Milestones

  • 2025: Launched our beta platform with 500 initial users.
  • 2026: Partnered with 50+ law firms to expand our database of legal precedents.
  • 2027: Reached 100,000 active users globally.
  • 2028: Introduced AI-powered document drafting and real-time consultation features.
